Contacts Screen

The list of your contacts from which to pick an address.

Note: your screen may look slightly different from the screenshots, depending on the version you have installed.

1

Picking a contact address

Just find and select the contact from the list that has the address you are after.

If the contact has multiple addresses, the a dialog will pop up to allow you to select which of the addresses you want to use.
